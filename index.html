About the Professor

Who is Prof. Shikha Srivastava?

Prof. Shikha Srivastava is a distinguished educator who has served as a Professor at MANIT (Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology), Bhopal and later at Mumbai University. Holding a BE and M.Tech in Electrical Engineering from MANIT Bhopal, she has guided hundreds of engineering students — from First Year to M.Tech level — through complex subjects with remarkable clarity and patience. Her deep subject knowledge and practical approach make even the most challenging engineering concepts accessible and engaging.

🎓 Qualifications

BE – Electrical Engineering, MANIT Bhopal · M.Tech – Electrical Engineering, MANIT Bhopal · Professor at MANIT Bhopal · Professor at Mumbai University · Expertise across undergraduate and postgraduate engineering curricula

💡 Teaching Philosophy

Prof. Srivastava believes that every engineering concept has a logical foundation. She builds understanding step by step — from fundamentals to advanced applications — ensuring students are exam-ready and conceptually sound.

🏆 Expertise

Specialises in Electrical Networks, Control Systems, Electrical Machines, Power Electronics, Industrial Electronics, Digital Logic Design and all related Electrical Engineering subjects at undergraduate and postgraduate levels.

📍 Location & Availability

Based in Mulund(W), Mumbai. Available for both Online and Offline classes. Timings are flexible — scheduled as per student availability. Reach out via phone or email to book a session.
